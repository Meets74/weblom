About Hiltner Law: A Federal Criminal Defense Lawyer for Ohio and West Virginia

Hiltner Law puts its focus exclusively on criminal defense.  Max Hiltner founded Hiltner Law in 2014 to remedy the lack of criminal defense firms.  Hiltner drew from his law school background of constitutional law and criminal law to develop his professional focus.  The law firm maintains a distinct emphasis on the role of trial lawyers.  While some attorneys may strive to avoid going to trial, the dedicated lawyers at Hiltner Law prepare themselves to go to trial at any point.  They realize how much their clients have on the line. Look to Hiltner Law to find a committed federal criminal defense lawyer. Maxwell Hiltner and Nicolette E. Drotos serve the Northeast Ohio community and beyond with their dedication to their roles as trial lawyers and to defending the rights of the accused.  

About Maxwell R. Hiltner, Esq. 

Max Hiltner is an accomplished trial lawyer.  He has distinguished himself as a leading federal criminal defense lawyer, particularly in the Northeast Ohio region.  He is certified to practice at the federal and state levels in both Ohio and West Virginia. Max Hiltner is a proud graduate of the University of Akron School of Law.  His law school experience, as well as his background with Gerry Spence’s Trial Lawyers College shaped his path to forming his own law firm in the Rubber City.   

About Nicolette E. Drotos

Nicolette E. Drotos is another graduate of the University of Akron School of Law.  Alongside Max Hiltner, Esq., she is a premier federal criminal defense lawyer for the region and beyond.  During her time as a Certified Legal Intern with the Legal Defender’s Office she noticed the injustice of the treatment of the accused within the legal structure.  This inspired her to go after her current career as a federal criminal defense lawyer, and her current position at Hiltner Law.
