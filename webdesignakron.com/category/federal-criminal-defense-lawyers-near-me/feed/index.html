About Maxwell R. Hiltner, Esq. 

Max Hiltner is an accomplished trial lawyer.  He has distinguished himself as a leading federal criminal defense lawyer, particularly in the Northeast Ohio region.  He is certified to practice at the federal and state levels in both Ohio and West Virginia.
