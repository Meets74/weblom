]]> What is Arboron

Arboron is composed of papers impregnated with melamine and phenolic thermosetting resins, consolidated under high pressure and temperature into dense, uniform sheets with good electrical and mechanical properties. The melamine surface of Arboron provides excellent chemical and abrasion resistance. It has a high strength-to-weight ratio and relative ease of fabrication which make Arboron sheets inexpensive and easy to transport all the while durable and long lasting. Electrical

  • Electrical control and test panels
  • Phase barriers
  • Bus bar insulation

Mechanical

  • Stage flooring
  • Die stock
  • Conveyer applications
  • Needle trade templates
  • Non-conductive workbenches
  • Transportation industry panels
  • Drill jigs and fixtures
