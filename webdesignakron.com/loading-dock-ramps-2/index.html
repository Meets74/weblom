Copperloy’s Loading Dock Ramps: Maximizing Space, Safety, and Productivity

Loading dock ramps from Copperloy by JH Industries feature an exclusive single cylinder structure.  Significantly, this unique design uses significantly less pressure than competitors’ models. Most importantly, Copperloy’s loading dock ramps provide easier operation, less wear and tear, faster raise times, and greater stability than the competition.  Copperloy’s proprietary loading dock ramps maximize space, safety, and productivity across a wide variety of industries. Above all, Copperloy by JH Industries prioritizes safety in the workplace. Take a look at the safety features of Copperloy’s loading dock ramps as shown below.  

  • 8-foot level off
  • 15-inch lip
  • Hydraulic pump
  • Low end plate
  • Durable deck grating
  • Safety curb
  • Positioning sleeve
  • Towbar
  • 18-inch solid/pneumatic tires

About Copperloy by JH Industries: Leading the Way in the Material Handling Equipment Manufacturing Industry
